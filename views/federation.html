Senegalese Rkateboard Federation (FSRS)

The Senegalese Roller Skateboard Federation is the national body for roller sports and skateboarding in Senegal. Our mission is to promote and develop these sports, and to support athletes in their pursuit of excellence.

Brief History

The history of the Senegalese Roller Skateboard Federation dates back to the early 2000s, when a group of passionate individuals decided to create an organization to promote and develop roller sports and skateboarding in Senegal. Since then, the FSRS has grown to become a respected institution, supporting athletes and organizing national and international events.
